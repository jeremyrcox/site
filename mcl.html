Memory Care Living

ROLE: DEVELOPER

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Memory Care Living is a senior living community that specializes in Alzheimer's and demntia care. I developed a custom responisve WordPress theme that includes some unique features. There is a virtual tour that allows a user to explore the facility by clicking on points of interest on a floorplan. The site adminitrator can upload the floorplan image, drag markers to points of interest and add content including text, images, photo galleries, and video. Another unique feature is the ability for users to create a life story timeline to celebrate the lives of loved ones by adding photos and stories to thier timeline. These life stories are also available on Facebook via a custom Facebook app.
